Program Enhancements


International

IBT Students are required to take at least two language courses (Grade 9 French and either Grade 10 French or Grade 10 German), and are encouraged to take more.

TLK is also proud to offer the only German courses in Peel, and works with our corporate sponsor, the Goethe Institute, to deliver an educational language-learning experience through resources and field trip opportunities.

Click here to learn more about the benefits of German course.

Why German @ TLK?

German provides IBT students with a gateway to a country that leads the way on key humanitarian and environmental issues and is on the cutting edge of research and development in fields such as:

  • International Business
  • Banking
  • Engineering
  • Aeronautics
  • Medicine
  • Pharmaceutical Industry
  • Green Ecofriendly Technology Development
IBT Alumni have been able to participate in international co-op experiences in their chosen fields because of the added advantage of an extra language that they had over their peers. Ultimately, this helped them move up to management positions in international companies.

Finally, Germany welcomes international collaboration and provides opportunities for subsidized work/study exchange programs.
Business

IBT Students are required to take at least two business courses (Grade 9 Business and Grade 10 Business, and are encouraged to take more. TLK offers a wide variety of business courses in Grade 11 and Grade 12 for specific career paths.

These courses teach students about business applications, innovation, ethics and morality, entrepreuneurship, and corporate social responsibility.

Technology

With the use of technology in the classroom, students can open doors to collaboration, professional tools and current information. They acquire the skills to think abstractly and complexly, show flexibility and clearly communicate.

TLK provides a variety of technology courses such as Technological Design, Communications Technology Computer Science, and Computer Engineering.

Click here to learn more about the technology enhancements of IBT.

Technology Enhancements

TLK is proud to offer the following technology enhancements for the IBT program:

  • 3D Printing and Wide Format Printing
  • VEX Robotics, Raspberry Pi and Arduino
  • Field Trips to International Manufacturing and Food Packaging Businesses
We also organize field trips to the Sheridan College Mechanical Engineering Labs, where students tour a robotics lab, solar lab, wind lab and H2O engine.

In grades nine and ten, IBT students receive a solid foundation in international studies, business and technology courses.

During their senior years, students may choose courses that lead to specialization in one of the three areas or continue to develop a broad perspective by continuing to study a variety of subjects.

Application Process

Students applying to the IBT Program at TLK must have their Grade 7 Report Card and Grade 8 Progress Report Card. The application consists of a written response relating to leadership and community involvement.

There is no interview or entrance exam.

The applications for the 2021-2022 school year have not been opened.
